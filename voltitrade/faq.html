Frequently asked questions

How do I verify my identity? +

Go to Dashboard → Security → Identity verification and follow the prompts to submit a government ID and a quick selfie. Most reviews complete within a few minutes.

What are the withdrawal limits? +

Limits scale with your verification level, from $2,000/day at Level 1 up to unlimited at Level 3. Check your current tier under Settings.

Is my crypto insured? +

95% of customer assets are held in cold storage and covered by our insurance fund against breaches of our own infrastructure.

How does staking APY work? +

APY is calculated daily based on network conditions and displayed before you confirm any staking position, with no hidden lock-up fees.

Can I use Vaultex on mobile? +

Yes — the platform is fully responsive, and native iOS and Android apps mirror every feature available on desktop.
